Meet Your Surgeon

Two decades of expert oral surgery & gentle dental care

MDS, OMFSBDSOral & Maxillofacial SurgeonKLE University, 2012

Dr. Supriya Bhandage is an Oral & Maxillofacial Surgeon and Dentist with over 20 years of experience, including 17 years as a specialist. She completed her MDS in Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery from KLE University, Belgaum, after earning her BDS from SDM College of Dental Sciences, Dharwad.

Known for being gentle, patient and reassuring, she explains every step clearly so you feel at ease. From routine treatments to complex oral & maxillofacial surgery, every plan is built around your comfort and long-term oral health.
